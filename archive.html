2020 Invitational Results and Letter from TAMU Scioly

Posted on February 08, 2020 by Tamu Scioly

Howdy!

The Texas A&M University Science Olympiad Society hosted an invitational competition on February 08, 2020. The results for that tournament are presented below. A full screen version may be accessed here.

We would like to thank all of the volunteers, organizations, and teams who made this tournament possible. Here are a few of the organizations that went into making this tournament go so smoothly: W5AC, the Texas A&M American Chemical Society Chapter, the Texas A&M Science Outreach and Women's Programs Office, and the Texas A&M FYP Chemistry Program.

We would also like to extend our thanks and our congratulations to all of the schools that participated. Some of the exams were quite difficult, but the teams present at the tournament still performed well on those exams! We thank you for bearing with us for this first time around and ask that you reach out if you have any suggestions on how to improve for next year.
