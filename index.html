Who are we?

The TAMU Science Olympiad Society is an undergraduate and Science Olympiad Alumni-led nonprofit organization in increasing interest in STEM and providing recognition for outstanding achievements in team events. Annually, we are excited to give back to the Science Olympiad community with organizing Division B Invitational and helping out with Texas State Science Olympiad Competition.
